Open Command Prompt:

Press Win + R, type cmd, press Enter.

Go to your emsdk folder:

cd C:\Users\User\OneDrive\Documents\emsdk


Activate Emscripten:

emsdk_env.bat


You should see:

Setting up EMSDK environment...
Adding directories to PATH...


Go to your project folder:

cd C:\Users\User\OneDrive\Documents\GameProject\HighwaySurvival


Compile your game:

emcc -o HighwaySurvival.html main.cpp -Wall -std=c++14 -D_DEFAULT_SOURCE -Wno-missing-braces -Wunused-result -Os -I. -I C:/raylib/raylib/src -I C:/raylib/raylib/src/external -L. -L C:/raylib/raylib/src -s USE_GLFW=3 -s ASYNCIFY -s TOTAL_MEMORY=67108864 -s FORCE_FILESYSTEM=1 --preload-file Graphics --preload-file Sounds --shell-file C:/raylib/raylib/src/shell.html C:/raylib/raylib/src/web/libraylib.a -DPLATFORM_WEB -s "EXPORTED_FUNCTIONS=['_free','_malloc','_main']" -s EXPORTED_RUNTIME_METHODS=ccall
